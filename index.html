About us

Our vision statement “EXPLORE, EXPERIMENT, EXCEL” reflects our understanding and beliefs. Children are given lots of opportunities to Explore their environment, follow their interests, Experiment new things and activity based learning will help them to Excel in their life.

We are a team of experienced, eminent and resourceful educationists, who believe in working hand in hand in collaboration with the parents to nurture the kids with passion, love and creativity. We are committed to provide safe and secured environment for the holistic development of every child.

Radiant Kidz provides spacious and well ventilated area for playing, state-of-art infrastructure, colourful and vibrant environment with proficient staff. We offer Daycare, Playgroup, Nursery and multiple educational and recreational facilities for children.

Professional Educators

All of our educators are qualified and experienced in providing quality care and education to your loved ones. Our Early Learning teacher is on-site 5 days per week and teaches our Preschool Program and over sees all of our centre’s academic services.

Small Class Size

Our Services

Services

Radiant Kidz is a latest venture introduce by us. We provide a stimulating, safe and happy environment for children aged between 0 – 10 years. We are open from 7:00AM to 6:00PM Monday to Friday, 50 weeks per year.

We provide optimal stimulating environment for children to express and learn with interest, fun and moral values. We intend that all children achieve their potential and become independent life-long learners. We also inculcate cultural values, secular approach and respect for humanity and environment. We impart innovative education practices from across the globe in fully inclusive environment for the overall development of each and every child. Children will be taught how to think, and not what to think.

As an added convenience, Radiant Kidz also provides healthy, nutritious meals for all children. Meals are prepared and cooked on site daily. We cater for kids with special dietary and/or religious requirements.

Radiant Kidz

Nursery Room

Educators in the Nursery room will plan, implement and evaluate activities for children’s learning based on the Early Years Learning Framework.

This framework conveys the highest expectations for all children’s learning from birth to five years through the following five learning outcomes:

  • Outcome 1: Children have a strong sense of identity.
  • Outcome 2: Children are connected with and contribute to their world.
  • Outcome 3: Children have a strong sense of wellbeing.
  • Outcome 4: Children are confident and involved learners.
  • Outcome 5: Children are effective communicators.


DayCare

Here at Radiant Kidz, we value the importance of learning through play where we believe young children learn best through their play experiences. Through play, children have an opportunity to make discoveries, explore resources and build on their imagination & creativity. Our programs are responsive to this and are tailored to each child’s needs and level of development. Our educators closely observe and document each child’s learning, they participate in their play experiences, extend their thinking, scaffold their ideas and intentionally introduce them to new learning.

Play also highlights children’s interests, both individual and peer interests, with these interests respectfully acknowledged and investigated further.

Our educational programs reflect this along with the principals and practices of the National Early Years Learning Framework which guides our educational and care programs.
